# Customer Intelligence & Business Decision Engine

## Project Overview

Customer Intelligence & Business Decision Engine is a real-world SQL business analytics project designed to simulate the work of a Data Analyst in an e-commerce company.

The goal of this project is to analyze customer, product, and order data using PostgreSQL to answer meaningful business questions and support data-driven decision making.

Instead of writing random SQL queries, this project focuses on solving practical business problems through structured SQL analysis and clear business insights.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Identify the highest-value customers.
- Evaluate product and category performance.
- Measure sales across different regions.
- Detect inactive customers.
- Generate business recommendations using SQL.

## Business Questions

This project aims to answer questions such as:

1. Who are the highest-value customers?
2. Which products generate the highest revenue?
3. Which product categories perform best?
4. Which regions contribute the most sales?
5. Which customers are becoming inactive?
6. Which products require restocking?
7. What is the average order value?
8. How do monthly sales change over time?
9. Which products have low demand?
10. What actions should management take?
