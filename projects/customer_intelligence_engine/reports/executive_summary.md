# Executive Summary

## Project

Customer Intelligence Engine using PostgreSQL

## Overview

The Customer Intelligence Engine analyzes retail transaction data from the Superstore dataset. The project demonstrates database design, SQL-based business analysis, and reporting using PostgreSQL.

## Dataset

- Records: 9,994
- Customers: 793
- Products: 1,862
- Orders: 5,009

- Total Orders

- Sales by Category
- Profit by Category
- Sales by Region
