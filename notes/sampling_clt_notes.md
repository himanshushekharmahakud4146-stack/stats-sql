# Sampling and Central Limit Theorem

## Population
The complete group of data points.

## Sample
A subset selected from the population.

## Sampling Distribution
Distribution formed by repeatedly taking samples and calculating a statistic.

## Central Limit Theorem
As sample size increases, the distribution of sample means becomes approximately normal.

## Why CLT Matters
- Foundation of statistical inference
- Used in confidence intervals
- Used in hypothesis testing
- Widely used in machine learning and data science
